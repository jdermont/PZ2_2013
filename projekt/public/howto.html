Tezauryzacja - HOW TO PLAY?

The game is simple - collect as many items as you can and give them to NPC. You do the more quest, you get the more points. You can trade desired items with players.
The game mechanics is pretty simple - you move using arrows, you trade using numbers and T and give item to NPC pressing Q.
For each quest you get of course points and your character is faster. That gives you some advantage over other players.
Game user interface is explained below:

press arrows to move player
press Q to make quest for NPC
press [1, ..., 6] to move item to trade or inventory
press T to make trade between players

Now you can close this tab and enjoy the game!
